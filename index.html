The 10 Principles

Tape these to your bathroom mirror. When the plan feels overwhelming, fall back to these.

  1. Strength training drives fat loss at 44 better than endless cardio — it preserves muscle and bone density.
  2. Walking is your secret weapon. Hit 10,000 steps daily — separate from gym workouts.
  3. Protein at every meal. Aim for a palm-size portion (chicken/paneer/eggs/daal) at each main meal.
  4. Sleep 7–8 hours. Below this, your body holds on to belly fat. Non-negotiable.
  5. Hydration: 3–4 litres of water. Most 'hunger' between meals is thirst.
  6. Weigh weekly — same day, morning, after toilet, before food. Daily weighing causes anxiety, not insight.
  7. Measure waist monthly with a tape. More honest than the scale.
  8. Get blood work done before starting: fasting glucose, HbA1c, lipid panel, Vit D, B12, TSH.
  9. Alcohol slows fat loss massively. Easy choice for you given your faith — keep it that way.
  10. The plan only works if you do it 80% of the time for 12 weeks. Don't chase perfection — chase consistency.

What to eat, what to limit, what to avoid

✓ Eat freely

  • Egg whites, whole eggs (1–2 yolks/day)
  • Chicken breast, fish (rohu, basa, salmon), chicken keema
  • Mutton — lean cuts, paya/yakhni soup (max 1× per week)
  • Paneer (low-fat), low-fat dahi, chaas, hung curd
  • All daals: moong, masoor, toor, chana, rajma, sprouts
  • Bhindi, lauki, baingan, palak, methi, gobhi, capsicum, tomato, kheera, mushroom
  • Whole-wheat chapati (1–2 per meal), multigrain roti
  • Brown rice / regular rice — 1 small katori max, once per day
  • Almonds, walnuts, pistachios (10–15 per day total)
  • Whole spices: haldi, jeera, dhania, hing, ajwain, dalchini, kali mirch
  • Lemon, mint, coriander, ginger, garlic, green chilli

~ In moderation

  • Biryani, pulao, naan, paratha — 1× per week, small portion
  • Mutton korma, nihari, rogan josh — 1× per fortnight
  • Mithai (jalebi, gulab jamun, peda) — 1 small piece, 1× per fortnight
  • Refined oils & ghee — total 2 tsp per day max
  • Cashews — small handful, occasional
  • Mango / lychee / chikoo — 1 small portion, occasional

✗ Avoid for 12 weeks

  • Sugar in chai/coffee — switch to no sugar (use cinnamon for warmth)
  • Sugary drinks: Frooti, Pepsi, Maaza, packaged juice — replace with chaas, nimbu pani (no sugar), green tea
  • Maida — naan, bhature, white bread, biscuits, pasta
  • Deep-fried street food: samosa, kachori, pakoda, bhajiya (Sarafa Bazaar — max 1× per month)
  • Late-night eating after 10 PM
  • Bhutte ka kees + cream, malai-heavy curries, Indori sev poha drowned in oil

Supplements (Optional)

None are required. They top up specific gaps. Pick brands with COA available — MuscleBlaze, ON Gold Standard, GNC are reliable in Indore.

Whey protein

1–2 scoops/day (training days)

Convenient protein top-up to hit 180 g target

Creatine monohydrate

5 g/day, every day

Most-researched supplement on earth. Safe. Builds strength and lean mass.

Vitamin D3

2,000 IU/day

80%+ of Indians are deficient; affects mood, strength, immunity

Multivitamin

1/day

Insurance against micronutrient gaps during a calorie deficit

Fish oil (Omega-3)

1 g EPA+DHA/day

Skip if you eat fish 3× per week. Otherwise helpful for joint & heart health.

Methi seed water

1 tsp seeds soaked overnight, drink in AM

Traditional, free, mild benefit for blood sugar & digestion

⚠ Red flags — see a doctor

  • Chest pain, tightness, or pressure during exercise — stop and see a cardiologist
  • Severe dizziness or fainting
  • Resting heart rate over 100 bpm regularly
  • Sudden shortness of breath disproportionate to effort
  • Snoring + daytime sleepiness — get screened for sleep apnea (common at 114 kg)
  • Knee, hip or lower back pain that lasts > 3 days — rest and consult physio
  • BP not checked in last 6 months — get it done in week 1
